# Privacy Policy for KD Web Element Hider

Last updated: June 27, 2026

KD Web Element Hider (the "Extension") is committed to protecting user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Extension handles information.

## 1. No Data Collection and Transmission
The Extension operates entirely locally on your personal device.
- **No Personal Data:** We do not collect, store, track, or share any personally identifiable information, such as names, email addresses, or identification numbers.
- **No Browsing History:** We do not track, collect, or store the list of webpages you visit.
- **No Remote Transmission:** The Extension does not transmit any data off your device. There is no remote logging, analytics tracking, or external server communication.

## 2. Local Storage
All configuration rules (element-hiding selectors) and user interface preferences (collapsed/expanded sidebar state) are stored strictly locally on your machine using Chrome's secure `chrome.storage.local` and `chrome.storage.session` APIs. This data remains under your sole control at all times and can be deleted instantly by clearing the rules inside the Extension dashboard or uninstalling the Extension.

## 3. Policy Changes
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be made public by updating this file in the official repository.

## 4. Contact
For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer at [khvichadev.com](https://khvichadev.com).
